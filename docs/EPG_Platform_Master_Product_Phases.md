# Enterprise Project Governance Platform (EPG Platform)
# Master Product Development Phases

## Product Motto
**Standardize. Govern. Audit. Deliver.**

## Product Vision
To build an enterprise-grade governance platform that becomes the operating system for project delivery, ensuring every project follows standardized, auditable, compliant, and intelligent workflows.
